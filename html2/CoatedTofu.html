    • Coated Tofu

    The tofu made by pointing with water from the underground well in Shiping overcomes the shortcomings of using a single coagulant to make tofu, but it also combines the advantages of each single coagulant to improve the dehydration and rancidity of the traditional coagulant. , The defect of unstable quality has greatly improved the taste, quality, quality and safety of Shiping tofu. Unique in quality, tenderness, deliciousness, and strong toughness, businessmen hold one end of the tofu and gently shake it to sell it without breaking it. Shiping tofu is famous for its fineness and smoothness, strong chewing, unusual fragrance and rich local flavor. After being baked and fried, the tofu gradually becomes smooth and plump, and the color turns golden. When you break it open, you can see that the cooked tofu is densely covered with pores and full of fragrance.

    Cooking methods

    1. Wash the tofu and dry it, and add an appropriate amount of base oil to the pot, and the amount of oil is a little more.
    2. Fry the tofu until the surface is golden, remove it, and put it on oil-absorbing paper to absorb the oil on the surface.
    3. Fry the peanuts in a frying pan.
    4. Use a mortar to crush peanuts.
    5. Put the tofu bubbles into a basin, and add millet hot rings.
