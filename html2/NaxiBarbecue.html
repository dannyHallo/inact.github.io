    • Naxi Barbecue

    Naxi Barbecue is a popular dish from Yunnan. It's made by skewering meats, vegetables, and tofu, and then grilling them over an open flame. The marinade is made with a variety of herbs and spices, giving the dish a distinct flavor. It's often served with a side of sticky rice or noodles, and is a great option for those looking for a quick and tasty meal while exploring the streets of Yunnan.
    "Naxi Barbecue" is the favorite of Naxi people. It is a traditional caravan dish popular on the Ancient Tea Horse Road in Lijiang. Slowly roasted carefully. It can be baked directly or baked half-cooked and then stored. When you want to eat it, you can deep-fry it and then add mint to fry it.

    Eating method

    Naxi barbecue looks oily and greasy, but it tastes golden and soft, the fat meat is fat but not greasy, and the lean meat is tender and fragrant. The local people's eating Naxi barbecue is also very characteristic, that is, it is served with mint, the red and white meat is embellished with green mint, which is pleasing to the eye, and the local special chili noodles are mixed with it. The taste is probably unforgettable for a long time.

    BBQ method:

    The way of Naxi barbecue is very local. It uses an oven shaped like a water tank. A brazier filled with chestnut charcoal is placed at the bottom of the tank. A circle of hooks for hanging meat is tied with iron wires around the oven. Slowly and carefully roasted. It is very important to control the heat when grilling meat, it is better not to be burnt or tender.
