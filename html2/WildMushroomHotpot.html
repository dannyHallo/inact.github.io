    • Wild Mushroom Hotpot

    Wild Mushroom Hot Pot is a unique delicacy in Yunnan. Chuxiong Wild Mushroom Hot Pot is one of the six famous foods in Yunnan. The ingredients are ecological and delicious, and it is the favorite of countless diners. Yunnan's complex topography, diverse forest types, soil types, and unique three-dimensional climate conditions have bred a wealth of wild edible fungus resources, with many types, wide distribution, and large yields.
    Wild mushrooms are unique wild edible mushrooms in Yunnan Province. They grow in a three-dimensional climate with complex topography and landforms at an altitude of 2000-4000 meters. Wild edible fungi in Yunnan are divided into two classes, eleven orders, thirty-five families, ninety-six genera, and about 250 species, accounting for more than half of the world's edible fungi and one-third of China's edible fungi two. In September 2018, it was rated as one of the top ten classic dishes of Yunnan in "Chinese Cuisine".

    Cooking methods

    Slaughter the hen, dissect off the viscera, wash it, chop it into 2 cm chicken pieces, put it in a pan with chicken oil, eggs, and chicken gizzards, add water to boil to remove the blood foam, change the water, add ginger pieces, and stew until chopsticks can be inserted into the chicken. Add salt , Wild Mushroom Chicken Soup Hot Pot Base, Pepper Noodles, and Scallions can be cooked together with various wild mushrooms.

    Nutritional value:

    The wild mushroom hot pot soup base is slightly cloudy, but it tastes delicious, much more fragrant than it smells, and it also has health benefits such as calcium supplementation. Fungi plants concentrate the essence of the earth, rich in amino acids, multivitamins, folic acid, lactic acid and other substances, which can promote human metabolism, regulate human physiological functions, and enhance immunity.
