    Welcome to Lijiang

    Lijiang City is located at the junction of Yunnan-Guizhou Plateau and Qinghai-Tibet Plateau. The terrain is high in the northwest and low in the southeast. The highest point is the main peak of Yulong Snow Mountain. Lijiang City is a multi-ethnic inhabited place, in addition to the Han nationality, a total of 12 ethnic minorities, 22 kinds of ethnic minorities.

    Lijiang is the only prefecture level city in China with three World Heritage sites at the same time. It is rich in tourism resources, with 104 scenic spots, including Lijiang Ancient City, Yulong Snow Mountain, Tiger Leaping-Gorge, Laojun Mountain, Shuhe Ancient Town and other well-known tourist spots. It has been rated as China's Top 100 tourism competitiveness cities, China's Top ten leisure cities , China's Outstanding tourism cities, China's top ten attractive cities and other honorary titles, and has been successfully established as a national Garden City, a national health city, and a national water-saving city. In 2021, Lijiang achieved a regional GDP of 57.05 billion yuan.
