    Welcome to Lijiang

    Lijiang City is located at the junction of Yunnan-Guizhou Plateau and Qinghai-Tibet Plateau. The terrain is high in the northwest and low in the southeast. The highest point is the main peak of Yulong Snow Mountain. Lijiang City is a multi-ethnic inhabited place, in addition to the Han nationality, a total of 12 ethnic minorities, 22 kinds of ethnic minorities.

    Lijiang City is located in the famous intersection of Yunnan, Sichuan and Tibetan cultures. Various cultures blend and coexist, and various ethnic groups live in harmony, giving birth to a colorful, magical and splendid ethnic culture.

    Traditional Festival

    The Yi, Bai, Naxi, Hani, Lisu, Lahu, Pumi and other ethnic minorities in Yunnan have a common festival - Torch Festival. The Torch Festival of Yi nationality has the largest number of people, the largest scale and the highest popularity.
