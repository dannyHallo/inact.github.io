    Welcome to Lijiang

    Lijiang City is located at the junction of Yunnan-Guizhou Plateau and Qinghai-Tibet Plateau. The terrain is high in the northwest and low in the southeast. The highest point is the main peak of Yulong Snow Mountain. Lijiang City is a multi-ethnic inhabited place, in addition to the Han nationality, a total of 12 ethnic minorities, 22 kinds of ethnic minorities.

    “Magnificent Cangshan”

    Cangshan

    Hsiangzhi saga 19 peak, from the north and south, continuous, towering in the west bank of Erhai Lake. Cangshan Mountain, the permanent master of the western Yunnan plateau, the favored child of heaven, which is hidden in the clouds and mist, appears particularly beautiful and majestic under the snow and ice. "Red day snow does not melt." Cangshan, with snow all year round, has a stream between every two peaks, flowing from top to bottom, along the east, slowly gathering, giving birth to the pearl of the plateau - Erhai Lake.

    Torch Festival of Yi Nationality

    The Yi, Bai, Naxi, Hani, Lisu, Lahu, Pumi and other ethnic minorities in Yunnan have a common festival - Torch Festival. The Torch Festival of Yi nationality has the largest number of people, the largest scale and the highest popularity. It is also the most solemn festival of the Yi people. Torch Festival content program is: during the day people drink to celebrate the festival and wrestling, bullfighting activities. In Wuding County, Luqianjia County and other places, the Yi people also carry out archery, horse racing, swing and other activities.
