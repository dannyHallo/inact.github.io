Lijiang

Lijiang City is located at the junction of Yunnan-Guizhou Plateau and Qinghai-Tibet Plateau. The terrain is high in the northwest and low in the southeast. The highest point is the main peak of Yulong Snow Mountain. Lijiang City is a multi-ethnic inhabited place, in addition to the Han nationality, a total of 12 ethnic minorities, 22 kinds of ethnic minorities.

Lijiang is the only prefecture level city in China with three World Heritage sites at the same time. It is rich in tourism resources, with 104 scenic spots, including Lijiang Ancient City, Yulong Snow Mountain, Tiger Leaping-Gorge, Laojun Mountain, Shuhe Ancient Town and other well-known tourist spots. It has been rated as China's Top 100 tourism competitiveness cities, China's Top ten leisure cities [3], China's Outstanding tourism cities, China's top ten attractive cities and other honorary titles, and has been successfully established as a national Garden City, a national health city, and a national water-saving city. In 2021, Lijiang achieved a regional GDP of 57.05 billion yuan.

美丽丽江

丽江市位于著名的滇川藏文化交汇地，多种文化交融并存、多种民族和睦相处，孕育了丰富多彩、神奇灿烂的民族文化。各民族在语言文字、神话传说、音乐舞蹈、文学艺术、宗教信仰，婚姻、丧葬、生育、节庆、饮食、服饰、待客、礼仪、娱乐活动以及心理素质、生态环境等方面都保留了自己独特的个性和多姿多彩的风格。各民族有众多的特有节日，如纳西族的正月十五棒棒节、三朵节，彝族的火把节，傈僳族的阔时节，普米族的吾昔节，摩梭人的转山节和他留人的粑粑节等。
