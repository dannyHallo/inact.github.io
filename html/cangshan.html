    • The ends of the Earth-Cangshan

    The remotest corners of the world - Cangshan has nineteen peaks, from the north and south, continuous, towering in the west bank of Erhai Lake. Cangshan Mountain, the permanent master of the western Yunnan plateau, the favored child of heaven, which is hidden in the clouds and mist, appears particularly beautiful and majestic under the snow and ice. "Red day snow does not melt." Cangshan, with snow all year round, has a stream between every two peaks, flowing from top to bottom, along the east, slowly gathering, giving birth to the pearl of the plateau - Erhai Lake. These nineteen peaks and eighteen streams not only constitute the unique and colorful landscape of Cangshan Mountain, but also give birth to a long and brilliant Bai culture.

    Cangshan is not only a nature reserve, but also a scenic spot. In 1992, the local government built a new Jade Belt Cloud tour road at an altitude of 2,600 meters above sea level. Tour from the south of Malong Peak, north of Yingle peak, about 18 kilometers, Cangshan many scenic spots linked together, visitors here, can see Cangshan peak, stone, forest spring waterfall, can also look down on the magnificent scenery of Canger
