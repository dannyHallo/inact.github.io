    • Discover the Opportunities of Lijiang's Growing Economy

    Lijiang offers favorable investment opportunities

    Lijiang offers investors a range of advantages, including a strategic location, a well-developed infrastructure, and a growing economy. The city is situated in a key location in Southwest China, with easy access to major transportation hubs and trade routes. It boasts a modern and efficient transportation network, including an international airport and high-speed railway lines. Lijiang's economy is also on the rise, with a strong focus on tourism, agriculture, and high-tech industries. The local government has implemented favorable policies to encourage investment and entrepreneurship, making it an attractive destination for both domestic and foreign investors. With its favorable location, infrastructure, and economic prospects, Lijiang presents a promising investment opportunity for those seeking to tap into China's growing market.

    Fantastic natural environment

    As the only city in China with three world heritages, Lijiang regards its tourism industry as the pride of the city. Located at the junction of the Yunnan-Guizhou Plateau and Qinghai Tibet Plateau, adjacent to Jinsha River, the tributary of Yangtze River, the unique natural environment has provided Lijiang with rich tourism resources. Being one of the most renowned tourism cities in China, Lijiang always provides generous returns to tourism industry investors.

    Ecological agriculture

    Located at the Yunnan-Guizhou Plateau with traditional subtropical monsoon climate, the geographical environment determines the possible varieties of agricultural products in Lijiang. Dense river network makes traditional agriculture in Lijiang concentrated on fishing and aquaculture. By utilizing sufficient sunlight conditions, Lijiang vigorously develops ecological agriculture centered around fruits, medicinal herbs and flowers. In the past three years, the total output value of Lijiang's primary industry has increased by over 8% annually.

    Green industry

    As a city with a thriving tourism industry, protecting the ecological environment is crucial. Different from traditional high energy consumption and high pollution industries, Lijiang is building a green industrial system that meets the local ecological and environmental needs. This green industrial system mainly involves clean energy, digital industries, biopharmaceutical processing, and agricultural product processing, contributing greatly to the economic growth of Lijiang.

    Prosperous tourism industry

    With its beautiful natural landscape and colorful cultural environment, Lijiang has become one of the earliest cities in China to develop tourism industry. Correspondingly, the tourism industry has also changed the culture of Lijiang, natural landscape attracts young people who are tired of urban life, and the demand of young people has led to an increase in the number of bars, as well as many musicians seeking to sing in bars, including one of the most famous singer -- Zhao Lei. Now Bars and singers have bacome a cultural landscape of this city, providing a large amount of potential growth space for the tourism industry in Lijiang.

    Healthy industrial structure and sustainable development expectations

    Based on data published by the Lijiang Municipal Bureau of Statistics, even in the face of the severe economic situation in 2022, Lijiang's total economic output still maintains positive growth. The primary and secondary industries increased by 5.4% and 14% respectively year-on-year, and the contribution rate of manufacturing and clean energy to industrial added value exceeds 90%. With the lifting of epidemic prevention and control policies, the operating revenue of Lijiang's tourism industry has also been continuously improving. Healthy industrial system ensures the vast economic development potential of Lijiang.
