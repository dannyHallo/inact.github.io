    Jade Dragon Snow Mountain

    Jade Dragon Snow Mountain is a majestic mountain range located in Yunnan Province, China. It is part of the larger Hengduan Mountain Range and is considered to be one of the most beautiful natural attractions in the region. The mountain range gets its name from the fact that it resembles a jade dragon lying in the snow.
    The mountain range spans over 550 square kilometers and has a peak elevation of 5,596 meters. It is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna, including many rare and endangered species. The mountain range is also home to a number of ethnic minority groups, including the Naxi people, who have a rich culture and history.
    Jade Dragon Snow Mountain is a popular destination for tourists who come to enjoy the stunning natural beauty of the area, as well as to participate in a range of outdoor activities such as hiking, skiing, and horseback riding. The mountain range is also home to a number of scenic spots, including the Blue Moon Valley, the Glacier Park, and the Spruce Meadow.
    Overall, Jade Dragon Snow Mountain is a must-see destination for anyone visiting Yunnan Province or China. Its stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage make it a unique and unforgettable experience for all who visit.

    Tiger Leaping Gorge

    Come and explore the breathtaking beauty of Tiger Leaping Gorge, one of the deepest gorges in the world! This popular destination is a haven for hikers and nature enthusiasts alike. Legend has it that a tiger once leaped over the roaring waters of the Jinsha River at its narrowest point to escape from hunters, giving the gorge its name.

    Black Dragon Pool

    Black Dragon Pool, also known as Heilongtan, is a must-visit destination in Lijiang, China. This landmark pool is a true marvel of nature, with its crystal-clear waters reflecting the breathtaking Jade Dragon Snow Mountain. Surrounded by lush greenery and swaying willow trees, Black Dragon Pool is a tranquil oasis that will leave you in awe.

    Mufu Palace

    Experience the rich history of Lijiang Ancient Town at Mufu Palace, the top attraction. Once home to the top ruler of Naxi minority Mu Family, the palace's intricate decorations impressed the famous geologist Xu Xiake. Stroll around the palace to see stunning courtyards, carvings, and paintings that tell Lijiang's story. Witness Lijiang's history and culture firsthand at Mufu Palace.

    Blue Moon Valley

    Discover the stunning beauty of Blue Moon Valley, a must-visit attraction in Lijiang. Located at the foot of Jade Dragon Snow Mountain, the valley's crystal-clear blue water reflects the majestic mountain scenery, creating a picturesque view that takes your breath away. Blue Moon Valley is an unforgettable natural wonder that offers visitors a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.
