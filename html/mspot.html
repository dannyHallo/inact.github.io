    • Blue Moon Valley

      Located in Lijiang City, Yunnan Province. It is renowned worldwide for its towering and magnificent snowy mountains, azure jade like lake water, and majestic and beautiful canyon scenery. It is named for its azure lake water and crescent shaped valley. It is said to be the prototype of the Blue Moon Valley in the Lost Horizon written by British writer James Hilton.

    • The sun shines on the sparkling lake surface, making it even more beautiful at sunset. Blue Moon Valley can be said to be a must-have place to punch cards in Lijiang.
