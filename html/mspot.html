    Lijiang offers favorable investment opportunities

    Lijiang offers investors a range of advantages, including a strategic location, a well-developed infrastructure, and a growing economy. The city is situated in a key location in Southwest China, with easy access to major transportation hubs and trade routes. It boasts a modern and efficient transportation network, including an international airport and high-speed railway lines. Lijiang's economy is also on the rise, with a strong focus on tourism, agriculture, and high-tech industries. The local government has implemented favorable policies to encourage investment and entrepreneurship, making it an attractive destination for both domestic and foreign investors. With its favorable location, infrastructure, and economic prospects, Lijiang presents a promising investment opportunity for those seeking to tap into China's growing market.

    Experience the Tibetan costumes in Lijiang

    Lijiang City is located in the famous confluence of Yunnan, Sichuan and Tibetan cultures, where many cultures coexist and many ethnic groups live in harmony, giving birth to a rich and colorful and magical national culture. Come to Lijiang to experience the colorful and strong Tibetan customs! In a mysterious and ancient city, wearing Tibetan costumes can experience a strong visual feast.

    Blue Moon Valley

    Located in Lijiang City, Yunnan Province. It is renowned worldwide for its towering and magnificent snowy mountains, azure jade like lake water, and majestic and beautiful canyon scenery. It is named for its azure lake water and crescent shaped valley. It is said to be the prototype of the Blue Moon Valley in the Lost Horizon written by British writer James Hilton. The sun shines on the sparkling lake surface, making it even more beautiful at sunset. Blue Moon Valley can be said to be a must-have place to punch cards in Lijiang.
